Backup

Backing up and restoring a device is crucial for safeguarding your data and ensuring continuity in case of unexpected events like device failure, loss, or theft.

Windows 10/11:

  • Settings: Go to Settings > Update & Security > Backup.
  • File History Settings: Click on "More options" under Backup, then "Back up now" to start the backup immediately or adjust settings such as backup frequency and files to include/exclude.
  • External Drive: Connect an external drive and choose it as the backup drive.

Mac:

  • System Preferences: Go to Apple menu > System Preferences > Time Machine.
  • Select Backup Disk: Choose an external drive as the backup disk.
  • Options: Click "Options" to exclude specific items from the backup if needed.
  • Backup Now: Click "Backup Now" to start the initial backup.

IOS (iphone/ipad):

  • Use iCloud Backup: Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > iCloud Backup > Turn on iCloud Backup.
  • Alternatively, use iTunes or Finder (on macOS Catalina and later) to create a backup on your computer.

Android:

  • Google Drive: Settings > System > Backup > Backup to Google Drive (options might vary slightly depending on device and Android version).
  • Some manufacturers also offer their own backup solutions, like Samsung's Smart Switch.
