What is Cyber Resiliance?

Cyber Resiliance Is The Method Of Preparing And Learning How To Respond To Cyber Attacks.

Pillars of Cyber Resiliance

Prevention

The measures taken to reduce vulnerabilites and prevent cyber-attacks. Prevention including installing security software and regularly update software.

Detection

The ability to identify cyber threats and incidents as they occur. Detection relies on real-time monitoring, intrusion detection systems, and threat intelligence to recognise and alert about suspicious activities.

Response

The actions taken when a cyber incident is detected. An effective response plan includes containment, eradication, and recovery to minimise damage and prevent further damage.

Recovery

The process of restoring normal operations after a cyber incident. Recovery includes data restoration, system cleanup, and continuous monitoring to ensure that vulnerabilities are addressed to prevent future incidents.
