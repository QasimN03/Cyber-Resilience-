A Stong password is the first defence against attackers

A strong password is the cornerstone of digital security, acting as the first line of defense against unauthorized access to personal and sensitive information. It serves as a barrier, preventing cybercriminals from infiltrating accounts and networks, thus safeguarding valuable data, financial assets, and privacy. In an era dominated by online interactions and digital transactions. By incorporating a combination of letters, numbers, and special characters, and avoiding easily guessable information like names or birthdays, a strong password enhances resilience against brute-force attacks and hacking attempts.

Device Update

Updating device regularly is important to keep your device safe, running well, and up-to-date with the latest improvements. To update Your Operating system weather that be windows or mac.

Windows

Mac

IOS

  • Settings: Go to Settings > Update & Security > Backup.
  • File History Settings: Click on "More options" under Backup, then "Back up now" to start the backup immediately or adjust settings such as backup frequency and files to include/exclude.
  • External Drive: Connect an external drive and choose it as the backup drive.

Android

  • Check for updates: Go to Settings > General > Advanced > System Software Update. the device will check if there are any available updates for your device.
  • If an update is available, tap "Download and Install." You may need to enter your passcode if prompted.
  • The update process may take some time. Ensure your device is plugged in or has sufficient battery life to complete the update.
  • Once the update is complete, your device will restart. You may need to enter your passcode again.
